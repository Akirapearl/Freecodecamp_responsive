About him and the creation of Linux

  • 1969 - He was born in Helsinki, Finlandia
  • 1980 - Has his own first contact with a microcomputer, the Commodore, he is introducted to BASIC language
  • 1988 - Is admitted into Helsinki's University, where he did study computer science. He also came to know the Minix OS.
  • 1990 - Creates his own implementation to Minix based on the Design of the Unix Operative System. October 5th the first version of Linux is announced, capable of executing Bash and the GCC compiler.
  • 1992 - GPL License is adopted for Linux. Added the freedom to use Linux as a free open source software, alowing its limitless modification, copy, redistribution and usage.
  • 2005 - Git is created as a software for version control.
